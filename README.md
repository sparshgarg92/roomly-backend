Matterport turns your floor plan into a living, breathing 3D model of your home — in minutes, in your browser, for free.
Upload a photo of your floor plan and we instantly generate a navigable 3D space. Walk into any room, rotate the view, and start making it yours. Change wall colors, swap floor materials, and watch the space transform in real time.
Found a sofa you love on Amazon? Drop the link in and see it placed directly in your room, to scale, exactly where you'd put it. No guessing whether it fits. No imagining the color against your walls. Just see it.
Then talk to your house. Tell the AI — I want the living room to feel warm in winter, I'm putting my home office in the north bedroom, I need the kids' rooms to actually be quiet at night. It looks at your actual floor plan, maps heat distribution room by room, models WiFi signal strength based on your wall layout, and scores the acoustics of every space. All of it rendered as live color overlays on your 3D model.
You're not reading a report. You're seeing your home's performance, visually, before a single decision is final.

Features:
3D Floor Plan Rendering — upload any floor plan and walk through a fully navigable 3D model instantly
Material & Color Editor — swap wall colors, floor textures, and materials in real time with a procedural texture engine built from scratch
Amazon Furniture Placement — paste any Amazon product link and place it in your room to scale, with automatic white-background removal
AI Spatial Analysis — powered by K2V2, get per-room scoring on thermal comfort, WiFi signal strength, and acoustic performance
Live Overlays — AI analysis renders directly onto your 3D model as color-coded visual overlays, not text reports
